FH CDN - Content Delivery Network
What's a CDN?

A CDN (or a Content Delivery Network) is a networked website that holds files. It holds images, documents, folders, videos and just our general files. FH Development uses one in order to store all of our departmental icons for embeds and group things.

Our CDN

FH Development's CDN holds our images and videos. We hold the different logos, profile icons, campus images, banners and everything here in just one place. We will also be holding files like payouts and presentations here for the future when we need them. FH Development holds files here.

Public Access (A)

FH Development does not permit public access to our Content Delivary Network. We prohibit public access as this is a staff network system. Staff are the only people in which should and do have access to this area of our website. We host our CDN using a sub-domain of fh-development.xyz.
